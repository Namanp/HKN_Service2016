EECS Day

Local high school students got a glimpse into the facilities of UC Berkeley EECS and see the kind of work incoming students do in their classes. Specifically the event featured a tour of the department (including facilities, labs, and halls), a computer science lab, and an EE lab.

BearHacks

An educational hackathon geared towards high school students, especially those with little to no coding experience. For 6 hours, students had the opportunity to work on a project of their own with teammates, and current Berkeley CS undergraduates will be there to offer guidance.
